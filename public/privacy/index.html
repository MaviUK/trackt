2. Information we collect

Account information

When you create or use a BURGRS account, we may collect:

  • Your email address.
  • Your unique account identifier.
  • Authentication and session information.
  • Account creation and login dates.
  • Password-related authentication information.

Authentication is handled using our authentication provider. We do not store your password in readable form.

Profile information

You may choose to provide:

  • Username and display name.
  • Profile photograph, avatar or cover image.
  • Biography.
  • Date of birth.
  • Gender and country.
  • Creator biography, niche and tagline.
  • Website and social-media links.

Some profile information may be visible to other BURGRS users and visitors.

Television activity

We collect information about how you use BURGRS, including:

  • Shows added to your account and watchlists.
  • Seasons and episodes marked as watched.
  • Watch progress.
  • Show ratings and scores.
  • Reviews and ranked-show lists.
  • Shows you follow or are interested in.
  • Calendar and release preferences.

Social and user-generated content

Where you use social features, we may process:

  • Reviews, replies, comments and chatboard messages.
  • Creator posts and lists.
  • Follows and follower relationships.
  • Reactions and votes.
  • Notifications.
  • Reports concerning content or other users.

Public content may be visible to other users and may be shared outside BURGRS by those users. Avoid posting private, confidential or highly sensitive information in public areas.

Support and issue reports

When you report an issue or contact support, we may collect:

  • Your name, username and email address.
  • The subject and description of the issue.
  • Steps required to reproduce the issue.
  • Screenshots you upload.
  • The page from which the report was submitted.
  • Browser, device, user-agent and viewport information.
  • The date and time of the report.
  • Our correspondence with you.

Screenshots may contain information visible on your screen. Remove or obscure passwords, payment details, private messages and other sensitive information before uploading them.

Technical information

When you access BURGRS, we and our providers may receive:

  • Internet Protocol address.
  • Browser, device and operating-system information.
  • App version and session identifiers.
  • Pages and features used.
  • Error and diagnostic information.
  • Dates and times of access.
  • Security and abuse-prevention information.

Information received from others

We may receive information about you where:

  • Another user follows, replies to, mentions or reports you.
  • A user reports content you have posted.
  • An app-store provider sends technical information.
  • A service provider identifies a security or technical issue.

3. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Save your shows, watch history, ratings and preferences.
  • Display your profile and public contributions.
  • Operate reviews, comments, chatboards, follows and creator features.
  • Generate notifications.
  • Provide customer support and investigate issue reports.
  • Moderate content and enforce our rules.
  • Prevent spam, fraud, abuse and unauthorised access.
  • Diagnose errors and improve BURGRS.
  • Maintain the security, availability and performance of the service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Establish, exercise or defend legal claims.
  • Communicate important changes to the service or our policies.

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not currently use personal information for third-party behavioural advertising.

4. Our lawful bases

Under UK data-protection law, we must have a lawful basis for each use of personal information.

Contract

We rely on performance of a contract where processing is necessary to:

  • Create and operate your account.
  • Store your watch history and preferences.
  • Provide ratings, reviews, rankings and social features.
  • Deliver notifications and account functions.
  • Respond to support requests.

Legitimate interests

We rely on legitimate interests where necessary to:

  • Maintain and improve BURGRS.
  • Investigate bugs and service failures.
  • Protect users and secure the platform.
  • Prevent fraud, spam and abuse.
  • Moderate user-generated content.
  • Understand how features perform.
  • Protect our legal rights.

When relying on legitimate interests, we consider the impact on users and whether their rights override our interests.

Legal obligation

We may process or retain information where necessary to comply with applicable law, court orders, regulatory requirements or lawful requests from authorities.

Consent

We may rely on consent for optional activities such as:

  • Non-essential notifications.
  • Marketing communications.
  • Access to photographs, files or other device features.
  • Any non-essential tracking technology introduced in the future.

You may withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect processing that took place before consent was withdrawn.

5. Public information

The following may be publicly visible, depending on the features you use:

  • Username, display name, profile and cover images.
  • Biography, creator information and social links.
  • Reviews, ratings, comments and chatboard messages.
  • Creator posts and lists.
  • Ranked lists.
  • Followers and following information.
  • Other content you choose to publish.

Do not post information you do not want others to see, copy or share. Deleting content may not remove copies previously saved, quoted or shared by other people.

6. Who we share information with

We may share information with trusted providers that help us operate BURGRS.

Supabase

Supabase provides database hosting, authentication, file storage and related infrastructure.

Netlify

Netlify provides website hosting, deployment and server-function infrastructure.

Resend

Resend processes transactional and support-related emails, including emails generated from issue reports.

Television-data providers

We may use providers such as TMDB and TVDB to retrieve show, episode, cast and release information.

App-store and platform providers

Apple and Google may process information relating to app downloads, device diagnostics, notifications and store accounts under their own privacy policies.

Embedded-content providers

Creator posts may include services such as YouTube or TikTok. Those providers may collect technical information under their own privacy policies when embedded content is viewed or used.

We may also disclose information:

  • To professional advisers, insurers or auditors.
  • To investigate fraud, abuse or security threats.
  • Where required by law or a court order.
  • To protect the rights and safety of BURGRS, users or others.
  • As part of a sale, restructuring or transfer of the business.

7. International transfers

Some providers may process personal information outside the United Kingdom. Where information is transferred internationally, we take steps intended to ensure an appropriate level of protection. These may include UK adequacy regulations, the UK International Data Transfer Agreement, the UK Addendum to approved standard contractual clauses, and other contractual, organisational or technical safeguards.

8. How long we keep information

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

  • Account, profile and television activity information is generally retained while your account remains active.
  • Public posts and social content are retained until deleted, anonymised or removed through moderation.
  • Issue reports, screenshots and support correspondence may be retained for up to 24 months after the issue is closed.
  • Security, diagnostic and technical logs may be retained for up to 12 months.
  • Information required for legal disputes, fraud prevention or compliance may be retained for longer where necessary.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise information that is no longer required. Limited residual copies may remain temporarily in secure backups until those backups are overwritten.

9. Account deletion

You may request deletion of your BURGRS account and associated personal information by emailing info@burgrs.co.uk.

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before completing the request.

Certain information may be retained where necessary to:

  • Comply with the law.
  • Prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Protect other users.
  • Resolve disputes.
  • Enforce our agreements.
  • Maintain evidence of moderation or safety decisions.

10. Your rights

Depending on the circumstances, you may have the right to:

  • Be informed about how your information is used.
  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Request restriction of processing.
  • Receive certain information in a portable format.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Object to direct marketing.
  • Withdraw consent.
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office.

These rights are not always absolute and legal exemptions may apply. To exercise a right, contact info@burgrs.co.uk. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

You may also raise a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office.

11. Automated decision-making

BURGRS does not currently use personal information to make solely automated decisions that produce legal effects or similarly significant effects on users. Features that rank shows, recommend content or organise information do not make legal or similarly significant decisions about you.

12. Cookies and local storage

BURGRS may use cookies, local storage and similar technologies that are necessary to:

  • Keep you logged in and maintain account sessions.
  • Remember preferences.
  • Cache information so pages load faster.
  • Protect the service from abuse.
  • Support app functionality.

We do not currently use advertising cookies.

If non-essential analytics, advertising or tracking technologies are introduced, appropriate information and consent controls will be provided where required.

13. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures designed to protect personal information, including:

  • Authentication controls.
  • Restricted database access and row-level database security.
  • Encrypted network connections.
  • Private screenshot storage and time-limited links.
  • Access restrictions for administrative systems.
  • Monitoring and diagnostic controls.

No internet service can guarantee complete security. Keep your login details secure and contact us if you believe your account has been compromised.

14. Children

BURGRS is not intended for children under 13.

We do not knowingly allow children under 13 to create accounts. If we learn that information has been collected from a child under 13, we will take reasonable steps to delete the account and associated information.

Users aged under 18 should avoid sharing personal contact details or other sensitive information publicly.

15. Third-party websites

BURGRS may contain links to external websites, social-media services and content providers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. Review their privacy policies before providing information or using their services.

16. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy where:

  • BURGRS introduces new features.
  • Our service providers change.
  • Our data practices change.
  • Legal or regulatory requirements change.

The latest version will be published on the BURGRS website and made available through the app. Significant changes may also be communicated through the app or by email.
