Community Guidelines

Last updated: 13 July 2026

BURGRS is a place for people to track television, share opinions and discover what others are watching. These rules apply to every profile, review, reply, comment, chatboard message, creator post, list, image and other contribution made through BURGRS.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use Community Guidelines

1. Be respectful

You can strongly disagree with a review, ranking or opinion. Criticise the idea, show or argument rather than attacking the person behind it.

Do not:

  • Insult, humiliate, intimidate or repeatedly target another user.
  • Encourage others to pile on, dogpile or harass someone.
  • Use unwanted sexual comments or degrading personal remarks.
  • Deliberately disrupt conversations or follow someone around the service to provoke them.

2. No hate or discrimination

Do not attack, dehumanise, threaten, exclude or promote hatred against a person or group because of characteristics such as race, colour, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age or another protected characteristic.

Discussion of difficult subjects, fictional prejudice, historical events or discriminatory themes in television may be allowed when it is clearly contextual, critical or educational and does not promote hatred.

3. No threats, violence or dangerous behaviour

Do not post content that:

  • Makes a credible threat against a person, group or place.
  • Encourages, celebrates or assists real-world violence.
  • Shares instructions intended to cause serious harm.
  • Targets victims of violence or tragedy with abuse.
  • Uses graphic violent material mainly to shock or distress others.

Fictional violence may be discussed, but graphic images or descriptions should be relevant, proportionate and clearly contextualised.

4. Sexual content and exploitation

Do not post:

  • Pornography or explicit sexual content intended primarily for sexual stimulation.
  • Non-consensual intimate images or threats to share them.
  • Sexual exploitation, trafficking or solicitation.
  • Sexualised content involving, depicting or targeting anyone under 18.
  • Requests for sexual images from minors or attempts to groom a minor.

Mature themes from television may be discussed in a non-graphic, relevant and age-appropriate way.

5. Child safety

Any sexual exploitation or abuse of children is strictly prohibited.

Do not:

  • Share, request, describe or link to child sexual abuse material.
  • Sexualise a person who is or appears to be under 18.
  • Encourage secrecy, coercion or inappropriate private contact with a minor.
  • Share identifying information that places a child at risk.

We may preserve and report relevant information to the appropriate authorities where required by law or necessary to protect a child.

6. Self-harm and suicide

Supportive discussion and personal recovery stories are allowed. Do not encourage, glorify, instruct or pressure someone to harm themselves or take their own life.

Do not post:

  • Instructions, methods or challenges relating to self-harm or suicide.
  • Content mocking or targeting someone because of mental-health difficulties.
  • Graphic images of self-inflicted injury.
  • Messages encouraging a person to die or hurt themselves.

Where we believe there is a serious and immediate risk, we may take protective action and contact emergency services where appropriate.

7. Illegal activity, fraud and harmful instructions

Do not use BURGRS to:

  • Commit, organise or facilitate criminal activity.
  • Promote terrorism or violent extremist organisations.
  • Sell or distribute illegal goods, controlled substances or stolen property.
  • Run scams, phishing, fraud, financial deception or impersonation schemes.
  • Share stolen credentials, malicious software or instructions for unauthorised access.
  • Evade law enforcement or help another person conceal serious wrongdoing.

8. Protect privacy and personal information

Do not share another person’s private information without permission, including:

  • Home addresses, private phone numbers or personal email addresses.
  • Passwords, login details, financial information or identity documents.
  • Private messages or private images where there is a reasonable expectation of privacy.
  • Real-time location information that could place someone at risk.
  • Sensitive medical, family or employment information.

Publicly available information may still be removed where it is shared to intimidate, harass or create a safety risk.

9. No impersonation or deceptive identity

Do not:

  • Pretend to be another user, public figure, company or BURGRS representative.
  • Create an account intended to mislead people about who operates it.
  • Use another person’s photograph or identity without permission.
  • Falsely claim verification, authority, partnership or endorsement.

Fan, parody and commentary accounts must make their nature clear and must not mislead users into believing they are official.

10. No spam or manipulation

Do not:

  • Post repetitive, irrelevant or unsolicited promotional material.
  • Send mass messages or repeated unwanted replies.
  • Use bots or coordinated accounts to manipulate votes, reactions, ratings or rankings.
  • Buy, sell or exchange fake followers, engagement or reviews.
  • Post misleading links, clickbait or content designed to trick users.
  • Create multiple accounts to avoid restrictions or deceive the community.

11. Copyright and other rights

Only upload content you created or have permission to use. Do not post full copyrighted episodes, films, scripts, articles, images or other protected material without authorisation.

Short quotations, screenshots or clips used for genuine review, criticism or discussion may still be subject to copyright law. You are responsible for ensuring your use is lawful.

Rights holders can report alleged infringement to info@burgrs.co.uk and should identify the protected work, the reported content and the basis of the complaint.

12. Reviews, ratings and recommendations

Reviews and ratings should reflect a genuine opinion or experience. You must not accept payment, gifts or another benefit in exchange for a misleading review or undisclosed promotion.

Do not:

  • Post fabricated reviews or ratings.
  • Coordinate campaigns to artificially raise or lower a score.
  • Review-bomb a show for reasons unrelated to the content.
  • Present advertising or sponsorship as an independent opinion.

Where a post is sponsored or influenced by a commercial relationship, make that relationship clear.

13. Spoilers

Spoilers are allowed, but be considerate. Where a feature provides a spoiler warning or spoiler control, use it.

Avoid placing major recent plot details in usernames, profile images, titles or opening lines where users cannot reasonably avoid seeing them. Deliberately using spoilers to harass or upset others may be treated as abusive behaviour.

14. Keep content relevant

Post in the most appropriate place and keep discussions reasonably connected to the show, episode, list, review or topic.

Off-topic conversation may be removed where it overwhelms a thread, disrupts other users or is being used to evade moderation.

15. Reporting a problem

Report content or behaviour using any available in-app reporting control or email info@burgrs.co.uk.

A useful report should include:

  • A link or clear description of the content or account.
  • The reason you believe it breaks these Guidelines.
  • Relevant screenshots or context where appropriate.
  • Any immediate safety concern.

Do not misuse reporting tools to silence someone merely because you disagree with their opinion.

16. How we enforce these Guidelines

We consider context, severity, intent, harm, account history and whether behaviour is repeated. We may:

  • Take no action where content does not violate the rules.
  • Add a warning, limit visibility or remove content.
  • Restrict comments, chatboards, posting or other features.
  • Temporarily suspend an account.
  • Permanently close an account.
  • Prevent a suspended person from creating replacement accounts.
  • Preserve or disclose information where required by law.

Serious violations may lead to immediate action without a prior warning. Repeated lower-level violations may also result in permanent removal.

17. Appeals

Where appropriate, you may ask us to review a moderation decision by emailing info@burgrs.co.uk.

Include your account email or username, the decision you are appealing, why you believe it was incorrect and any relevant evidence. Repeated or abusive appeal submissions may not receive further responses.

18. Moderation limitations

BURGRS may use a combination of user reports, automated controls and manual review. We cannot guarantee that every violation will be detected immediately or that every moderation decision will be error-free.

These Guidelines do not create a promise that every piece of content will be reviewed before publication.

19. Changes to these Guidelines

We may update these Guidelines as BURGRS develops, new risks emerge or legal and platform requirements change. The current version will be published here with an updated date.
